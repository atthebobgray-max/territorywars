🌍 Territory Wars

📖 How To Play

🎯 Territory Mode

Capture the enemy core ⭐ or surround it to win.

🗺️ Land Mode

You have 2 minutes to capture as much land as possible. When the timer ends, the player with the most land wins.

🎲 Turns

Roll the dice to get moves.

🏆 Winning Countries

Winning a country colors it: Blue = Player Won Red = AI Won

🔄 Replay

If you play a country again, the map resets back to normal for that country.
