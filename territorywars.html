🌍 Territory Wars

📖 How To Play

🎯 Goal

Capture the enemy territory core ⭐ or surround it to win.

🎲 Turns

Roll the dice to get moves.

🟦 Your Land

Expand from your territory by placing tiles next to your own land.

🏆 Claiming Countries

If you win, the country becomes yours forever.

💀 Losing

If the AI wins, the land stays unclaimed until you replay and win.
